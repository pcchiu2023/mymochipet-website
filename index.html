What makes Mochi special

Not just another virtual pet. Mochi is powered by real AI that runs entirely on your iPhone.

Real AI Personality

Mochi doesn't follow scripts. A language model running on your device generates unique responses based on mood, memories, and personality traits that evolve over time.

Truly Private

Everything stays on your phone. No servers. No cloud. No data collection. Your conversations with Mochi are between you and Mochi — forever.

Works Offline

On a plane. In the subway. At camp. Mochi is always there because the AI lives on your device, not in the cloud.

Grows With You

Feed, play, talk, dress up. Mochi develops personality traits based on how you care for it. Neglect it and it gets dramatic. Love it and watch it bloom.

Dress & Customize

Hats, scarves, star glasses, backgrounds — collect outfits and accessories from the shop. Make your Mochi uniquely yours.

Free to Play

The full Mochi experience is free. Optional Best Friend pass ($2.99/mo) and cosmetic items support development. No pay-to-win. No ads. Ever.
